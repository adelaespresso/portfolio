05 — About

Adéla

I am an emerging filmmaker who loves experimenting with different forms of storytelling. My creative journey began with literature and writing before expanding into filmmaking, where I found a way to combine words, images and emotion.

Inspired by documentary, fiction and experimental cinema alike, I am interested in how film can capture fleeting moments, memories and human connection. Living and studying in Denmark for two years broadened my perspective and strengthened my interest in cross-cultural storytelling and collaboration.

As I prepare to study film at university, I continue to explore various cinematic forms, always seeking new ways to tell stories that resonate with audiences.
